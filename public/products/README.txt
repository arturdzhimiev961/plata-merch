## Image Requirements

For optimal display:
- Use high-quality images with good lighting
- Maintain a consistent aspect ratio (preferably 4:3 or 1:1)
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 500KB per image)
- Use JPG or WebP format for photos
- Recommended dimensions: 800x600 pixels or larger
